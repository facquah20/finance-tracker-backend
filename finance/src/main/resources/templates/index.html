What does FinTracker do?

Knowing how to manage your finances well is a key to achieving your financial goals. Don't beat yourself if you haven't had a good track record with managing finances. It's never too late if you want to get your money under control. It is with this that we introduce FinTracker .

FinTracker is a web application that helps individuals keep track of their income, expenses, and transactions. Below are operations you can perform on the app:

  • Add a new source of income
  • Keep your expenses in a well managed database for future reference
  • Record all your transactions
  • Provision of free reading materials on finance management
